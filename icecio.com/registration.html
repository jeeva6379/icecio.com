Registration


Guidelines for Final Documents Submission

All authors of the accepted paper need to carry out the following steps to submit the camera-ready documents:


1 – Final Updated Camera-Ready Paper:

✔ Authors should revise paper according to reviewer comments.

✔ The final manuscript length should be 12-15 pages.

✔ Author should use the conference paper ID for proceeding further in the payment gateway.

✔ The final manuscript should be sent along with its source document [Ms-Word/LaTex]


2 – Copyright Form

The authors should download the Copyright form mentioned below and it should be submitted along with the final documents.

✔ In the first page, please fill your paper title and complete author details.

✔ Only the corresponding author’s signature is required in the last page.


3 – Conference Registration

In order to complete the conference registration, each paper must be covered by an author registration. Single conference registration includes presentation and publication of 1 submission.
